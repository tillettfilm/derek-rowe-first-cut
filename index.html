Act 06 · The Lookbook

How it will look. How it will feel.

Working Title
"The Standard Made Visible"
Visual
Dark, controlled, premium and disciplined. Derek in striking and spacious environments: training spaces, live rooms, quiet preparation, coaching conversations, direct to camera, movement, stillness, detail, texture, sweat, focus, leadership and human proof. The camera should make him feel like a man with calculated standards. We are not trying to convince people he has them. We are showing them.
Pace
Purposeful and controlled. Not frantic. Not a motivational montage. Enough intensity to hold attention. Enough restraint to feel premium. The rhythm should feel confident, not desperate.
Sound
Grounded, atmospheric, powerful and ambient. The film should feel serious even without being loud. For website, LinkedIn and social use, the visuals need to carry authority whilst the audio compliments with raw sound.
Structure
Derek is the standard. Move into the problem his people face. Show the work, the discipline, the coaching, the environment and the transformation. Leave the viewer with one message: this is not another coaching brand. This is where serious people change.
